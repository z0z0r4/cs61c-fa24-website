Exam Info

The midterm will be held in-person on Monday, October 14th, 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM PT. There will be a single alternate exam on Tuesday, October 15th at 8AM PT that continues for the amount of time you need. As a reminder, the clobber policy will apply to all students, even if you are unable to take the midterm.

The final will be held in-person on Monday, December 16th, 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM PT. The alternate time exam will start at Monday, December 16th, 11:00AM PT. Alternate exams will only be offered for documented time conflicts, extenuating circumstances, and religious accomodations.

Final

If you have DSP accommodations or would like to request an adjustment to your exam (such as a left-handed desk, a seat in a specific room, or an alternate exam due to time conflicts), please fill out the final adjustments form by Friday, December 6th, 11:59 PM PT. We will respond to all requests submitted before this date by Friday, December 13th, 11:59 PM PT.

After this point, we cannot guarantee that we will be able to accommodate any alteration requests, and requests will be reviewed on a rolling basis. However, if you're feeling unwell or there's an emergency, please email cs61c@ directly.

Please only email cs61c@ for emergencies or if you're feeling unwell. Please do not post on Ed, since there's a higher chance of us missing your post.

Room and seat confirmations will be sent by Saturday, December 14th, 11:59 PM PT via email.

Alternate and Online Exams

If you have another exam conflict that directly overlaps with the main exam time, we are offering one alternate exam on Monday, December 16th, 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM PT. There will be no online exam offered for the final.

Final Scope

The final covers the following topics:

  • Lectures 0 through 34
  • Discussions 1 through 12
  • Labs 0 through 7
  • Homework 1 through 10
  • Projects 1 through 4

Materials only covered in the textbook are not within scope. However, materials not covered in lecture, but covered in discussion, lab, homework, or project are within scope.

Final Resources

The exam is closed-book, closed-note, closed-internet, with the following exceptions:

  • You can prepare and use up to 2 two-sided letter-sized study sheet for the final. You can reuse your midterm cheat sheets for the final.
  • Study sheets must be handwritten. Digitally handwritten notes may be printed and used.
    • We will take away any study sheets that do not follow this policy.
  • On the exam, you will be provided with the 61C reference card.

The midterm covers the following topics:

  • Every lab through Lab 4
